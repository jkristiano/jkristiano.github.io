Research

Interest

Quantum field theory of fluctuations generated during cosmic inflation.

Keywords: Cosmic Inflation, Cosmological Perturbation, Quantum Field Theory in Curved Spacetime, Cosmological Bootstrap, and Primordial Black Holes.
